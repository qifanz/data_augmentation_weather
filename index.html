Project Proposal

Problem statement

For instance if we want to train a deep learning model to recognize cars. We train this model with many photos of cars during sunny days. But what happen when the weather change and that the weather is snowy, rainy or foggy ? The model don't work anymore.

The idea is to do Data augmentation. Which means to train the model with fake photos of rainy, snowy or foggy days. The project would focus on : "how do we build fake photos of rainy days, foggy days or snowy days ?"

The goal is to take images ( already labeled ) and to transform these images to change the weather. With deep learning ( using GAN ). Researchers can have good results : https://www.youtube.com/watch?time_continue=1&v=e3aXvYIWIq0
